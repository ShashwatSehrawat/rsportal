Department of Mathematics

Welcome to the Department of Mathematics, IIT Hyderabad. From its inception to the present, the Department of Mathematics has evolved and grown in several directions. In the last five years, this change has been research output and the diversity of research pursued. Department is fast becoming a world class center for theoretical, applicable and interdisciplinary research. Maths department has young and dedicated faculty working with in pure and applied branches of Mathematics who actively collaborate with their counterparts from the other engineering departments. The department aims to foster eclecticism and excellence in mathematical education and research which is well poised between abstraction and applications.
To consolidate and sustain these achievements and to enable continued future growth we outline the Department’s goals:

  1. 1. To attract motivated and talented students to the master’s and doctoral programmes of the Department.
  2. 2. In research, to pursue collaborative programmes with highly reputed national and international institutions.
  3. 3. To provide a stimulating teaching environment for the undergraduate and graduate students of the other Departments within IITH.
  4. 4. To promote informed discourse on mathematics education at a national level and to popularise mathematics in society at large.

Areas of Interest:

The research pursued in the Department of Mathematics includes a fairly wide spectrum of interests in Number Theory, Functional Analysis, Operator Theory, Wavelets, Computed Tomography, Sparsity Seeking Optimization techniques, Convection in Porous Media, Fuzzy Logic Convective, Approximate Reasoning, Harmonic Analysis, Partial differential equations, Variational methods and Geometric analysis.
